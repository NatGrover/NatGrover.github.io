Digital 1 Projects

This is a gathering of some of my best projects from my digital 1 class

Project 1

Color Theory - Triadic

In this project, we chose specific color schemes using Adobe Color, and created artworks based on those. In this piece I used a triadic color scheme of yellow, cyan, and magenta, and created a creepy digital piece

Project 2

Photo Editing and Atmospheric Perspective

In this project, we had to take photographs and edit them in a manner that created atmospheric perspective. I made another creepy and entertaining piece, inwhich a larger version of me is hunting myself. Using the burn and add tools in photoshop helped to really bring my idea to life.

Project 4

Self Expression

In this project, we were to use photos and digital painting to create a piece that expresses who we are. I drew characters from my favorite childhood videogames alongside a picture of myself, heading towards one of the most peaceful scenes from my favorite videogame. I feel that the characters, scene, and photography, coe together to show how I've grown with these game.
