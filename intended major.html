Industrial Design

My Intended Major

My intended major in college is Industrial Design. The profession is essentially the creation of product ideas based on things like aesthetic, the consumer, social, and commercial attitudes. I want to work in this field, as I used to be a Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics student. Within the STEM curriculum, the projects and solutions were stiff, forced and unimaginitive. I wanted to do something different than what my teachers presented me. My father informed me of the profession, and took me to an Industrial Design firm so I could learn more about it. I became fascinated immediately. The projects they showed that really could do something, make a mark of some form, inspired me to pursue the profession. I'm very excited to enter my major next year!
